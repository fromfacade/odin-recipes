Mrs.Sigg's Snickerdoodles Recipe:

  • Prep time: 20 mins
  • Cook time: 10 mins
  • Total time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 48

Description:

Snickerdoodles are one of my favorite types of cookies. You have to try this top-rated snickerdoodle recipe. The light and fluffy cookies are sweetly specied with sugar and cinnamon, then baked to warm perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Recipe:

Step 1:

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

Step 2:

Make cookies: Beat sugar, butter, shortening, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl until smooth and creamy.

Step 3:

Whisk flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt together in a seperate bowl. Gradually mix dry ingredients mixture into the wet ingredients just until combined. Shape dough into walnut-sized balls.

Step 4:

Make Cinnamon-sugar: combine sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl or zip-top plastic bag.

Step 5:

Place dough balls in cinnamon-sugar and roll or shake until coated. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.

Step 6:

Bake in the preheated oven until set but not too hard, 8 to 10 minutes, switching racks halfway through.

Step 7:

Remove from the oven and immediately transfer to wire racks to cool.
